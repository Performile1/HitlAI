## Training Bonus Criteria

**What qualifies as "high-quality training test":**

1. **Company rating:** 4+ stars
2. **Detailed feedback:** Not just "looks good" - specific issues found
3. **Human verification:** You verified AI predictions (confirmed/missed/false positives)
4. **Used in training:** Test was included in a fine-tuning batch

**Example high-quality test:**
```
Test: E-commerce checkout flow
Issues found: 5 specific friction points
AI verification: Confirmed 3, found 2 AI missed, flagged 1 false positive
Company rating: 5 stars
Comments: "Very thorough, found critical payment button issue"
Result: ✅ Qualifies for $5 training bonus
```

**Example low-quality test:**
```
Test: Homepage review
Issues found: 1 vague issue
AI verification: None provided
Company rating: 3 stars
Comments: "Seems okay"
Result: ❌ Does not qualify for training bonus
```

## FAQ

**Q: Is this real equity or just "phantom equity"?**
A: Real equity. You'll own actual shares in HitlAI Inc. with voting rights and participation in exits.

**Q: What if I can't complete the minimum tests?**
A: You keep whatever equity you've vested. No penalties. But you won't vest the rest.

**Q: Can I sell my equity?**
A: After fully vested, yes - with company approval (standard ROFR). Or wait for exit/IPO.

**Q: What if the company fails?**
A: Equity becomes worthless (like any startup equity). But you keep all test earnings and bonuses.

**Q: Is the 40% revenue share really lifetime?**
A: Yes. Even if we change rates for new testers, you keep 40% forever.

**Q: Can I be both a Founding Tester and invest money?**
A: Yes! If you want to invest in our seed round, we'll give you priority allocation.

**Q: What happens if I refer a company that becomes a customer?**
A: 10% of their first year revenue (on top of referral bonuses).

**Q: Do I have to pay taxes on the equity?**
A: You'll receive a tax form (83(b) election recommended). Consult your accountant. We'll provide guidance.
